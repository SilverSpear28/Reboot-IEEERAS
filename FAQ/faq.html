Faq selection

Who all can register?

The hackathon is open to those who are interested in technology and innovation can register.

What is the registration fees?

Great hackathons are always free

How many members are required in a team?

You can have 1-5 people in a team.You need to create your own team before participating

How do I submit what I have made for the hackathon?

The hackathon will be conducted in 3 Phases.The first phase will be an online round where teams have to submit their idea abstracts.In the second phase,teams have to pitch their ideas.In the final phase participants have to demonstrate complete working models/prototypes of their projects.

Do we need to build a full product?

Only a prototype having some scope

What if I am hungry?

Eat Code Create will be the main objectives of hackathon.You will be provided with the tasty refreshments throughout the event.

Who will be the judges?

Faculties and Guest from some big companies

What will be the prizes for the event?

Prizes worth 15k,10k and 5k will be provided to the winning teams
