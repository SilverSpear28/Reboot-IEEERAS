WHY
REBOOT?

The main purpose of Reboot is to introduce feshers to the world of hacks and make them realise their true potential and the technical field that will appeal to them the most. Problem statements involving Healthcare, Security, Digital India, Education and Connecting India will be provided. It will be a 36-hours hackathon including sepaate sessions for team formation, idea presentation, product/prototype development, and final pitch. Participants will be judged on the basis of the following criterion: Originality, Presentation, Product quality, Code quality and Final presentation. We encourage freshers to come and participate along with their seniors ans experts from VIT and competitors from oter colleges as well.
