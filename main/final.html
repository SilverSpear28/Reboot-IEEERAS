Why
Reboot?

The main purpose of Reboot is to introduce freshers to the world of hacks and to help them identify their true potential and the technical field that appeals to them the most. Problem statements revolving Healthcare, Security, Digital India, Education and Connecting India will be provided. It will be a 36-hours hackathon including separate sessions for team formation, idea presentation, product/prototype development, and final pitch. Participants will be judged on the following criterion: Originality, Presentation, Product quality, Code quality and Final presentation. We encourage freshers to come and participate along with their seniors ans experts from VIT as well as with competitors from other colleges.

FAQ's

Who all can register?

This hackathon is open to all those who have keen interest in technology and innovations.

What is the registration fees?

Great hackathons are always free

How many members are required in a team?

You can have 1-5 people in a team.You need to 'form' your own team before participating

How do I submit what I have made for the hackathon?

The hackathon will be conducted in 3 Phases.The first phase will be an online round where teams have to submit their idea abstracts.In the second phase,teams have to pitch their ideas.In the final phase participants have to demonstrate complete working models/prototypes of their projects.

Do we need to build a full product?

Only a prototype having some scope

What if I am hungry?

Eat Code Create are the main objectives of this hackathon. You will be provided with refreshments throughout the event

Who will be the judges?

Faculties and Guests from some big companies will be judging your performance.

What will be the prizes for the event?

Prizes worth 15k,10k and 5k will be provided to the winning teams
